UBUD

Ubud is one of Bali's famous tourist destinations, located in the central part of the island. Known as the center of tourist, cultural and culinary destinations.

About Ubud

Why you should visit Ubud?

Ubud, Bali, is a popular tourist destination because it has many interesting things, such as natural beauty, spiritual tourism, culture and handicrafts.

Lot of choices

Ubud offers more than 25+ tourist destinations that appeal to visitors.

Affordable price

Ubud offers a wide range of accommodation options, from budget hostels to luxury resorts.

Easy access

Ubud is strategically located in the center of Bali, providing easy access to many tourist attractions.

nature

Stunning Natural Beauty and destination

Ubud is famous for its stunning natural landscapes, from the verdant Tegalalang rice terraces, to the lush tropical forests, to the serenely flowing rivers. It is perfect for those seeking tranquility and natural beauty.

RELAXATION

Spiritual and Relaxation Tourism

Ubud is a popular destination for spiritual retreats and relaxation. Many places offer yoga retreats, meditation and spas that utilize traditional Balinese healing techniques, making it an ideal place to rejuvenate the mind and body.

CULTURE

Bali Art and Culture Center

Ubud is the heart of Balinese art and culture. Here, you can find many art galleries, museums and traditional Balinese dance performances. A visit to Ubud Palace or watching Kecak and barong dances is an unforgettable experience.

ART & FESTIVAL

Handicrafts and Festival

Ubud is the center of Balinese art and culture, famous for its high-quality handicrafts, such as wood carvings, and paintings. In addition, Ubud also organizes major art festivals such as the Ubud Writers & Readers Festival and BaliSpirit Festival.

CULINARY

Local Culinary Experience

From traditional warungs to fine dining restaurants, Ubud offers a variety of culinary delights with authentic Balinese flavors that tantalize the taste buds.
